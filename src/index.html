by Maddie Nicolas

Info about the AidData dataset

The AidData dataset contains information about aid donations between two countries.

Each row represents one transaction between two countries with the following attributes:

  • Year: year of the commitment
  • Donor country providing the financial resource
  • Recipient: country or organization receiving the money
  • Commitment Amount USD: the total amount of financial resources provided
  • Coalesced Purpose Name: the purpose of the transaction

Visualization 1

  • How does the amount donated vs. amount received change over time across all countries?
  • Are there countries that mostly send or mostly receive and countries that have a similar amount of donations they receive and send?
  • Are there countries that change their role over time? That is, they used to mostly send donations and turn into mostly receiving donations and vice-versa?
  • Are there countries in which you can find a sudden increase ("peak") or a sudden decrease ("valley")?

Visualization 2

  • What are the top 10 purposes of disbursements (in terms of total volume) and how does their volume compare over time?
  • Are there types that have a much higher/lower volume overall among the top 10?
  • Are there types that increase or decrease their volume over time or that have sudden peaks or valleys?

Visualization 3

Focusing on a country that has a history of sending amounts to many other countries (pick one):

  • How does the set of countries it sends donations to change over time?
  • Are there countries that receive higher amounts in specific time periods?
